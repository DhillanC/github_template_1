# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

Contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Assume good intent while still asking for clarity.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing sensitive information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Insults, threats, or harassment.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Discriminatory comments or exclusionary behavior.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block contributors, or take other appropriate action to keep the project productive and respectful.

If this template is used for a public community project, replace this file with the project's official conduct policy and reporting process.
